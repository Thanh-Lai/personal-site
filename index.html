About Me

Hi, my name is Thanh! I am a Software Engineer in New York City. My most enduring trait is my curiosity and that shows in my willingness to learn. My hobbies include watching my beloved Manchester United, taking tranquil hikes, and long directionless strolls. Besides computer science, I like to seek out long meaningful conversations in a range of topics include animals, history, science, politics, and geopolitics.
